📚 Task Catalogue

Tasks are randomly sampled from the following catalogue:

  • Rescue Victim (High) — requires medical support or search and mapping
  • Clear Debris (Medium) — requires heavy debris removal
  • Deliver Medicine (Medium) — requires medical support
  • Map Building (Low) — requires search and mapping

🎯 Priority System

Tasks are prioritized by urgency level:

  • HIGH — Immediate threat to life, handled first
  • MEDIUM — Significant risk, address after high priority
  • LOW — Important but non-critical
